ABOUT US

Sky Community School is a private basic institution owned by Ing. James Mensah Yevunya, Mad. Francisca Elorm Kugbega, Mr. Edem Yao Kugbega and Mr. Lincoln Agyei. The school was started with seven children and has currently grown to providing education services to hundreds of children. Management is in charge of admissions at all levels and reserves the right to admit a child or otherwise without an explanation. An admission fee plus the term’s fees are payable in full as soon as a child is offered a place in the school. A child’s admission is deemed complete upon the settlement of all fees related to the admission and the endorsement of the contract form on behalf of the child. Signature on the admission form implies that all rules and regulations of the school have been read and understood. Parents and guardians must cooperate fully.


SKY COMMUNITY SCHOOL

The Best School in Central Region

Sky Community School, located in Fetteh-Kakraba, Central Region of Ghana, stands out as the best school in the region due to its unwavering commitment to the holistic development of its students and its exceptional approach to education.

HOLISTIC APPROACH:
Sky Community School's adherence to the motto "Head, Heart, Hand" showcases its dedication to nurturing well-rounded individuals. The school not only focuses on academic excellence but also places equal importance on character building, empathy, and practical skills development.

QUALITY EDUCATION:
The school's emphasis on developing the intellectual abilities (Head) of its students ensures that they receive a top-notch education. The teachers are highly qualified and dedicated to fostering critical thinking and a love for learning among the students.

ETHICAL FOUNDATION:
Sky Community School's focus on the emotional and moral development (Heart) of students equips them with a strong sense of ethics and compassion. This emphasis on values contributes to the students' personal growth and their ability to positively impact their communities.
